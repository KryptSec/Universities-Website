KSU is a growing community of Cyber Security Enthusiasts, Students and Faculty at Colorado State University in Fort Collins. We are centered around developing and enhancing specific skills through software development, vulnerability testing & analysis, forensics and more. KryptSec U is constantly trying new things while learning and sharing together and are actively seeking others interested.

Since we have formed in September 2018, we have developed our website and discord community, created community hacking challenges, developed our very own discord bot that accepts user flag submissions, and currently our international community team is placing in the top 100 teams on hackthebox. It is our goal to work as an efficiently functioning unit, everyone participating in something aimed towards the betterment of themselves and the members of KryptSec. We work hard and we dream big, but most importantly, we have a blast doing it.

To see what we are all about come hang out with us in Discord, follow us @Krypt_Sec, vist our CSU RamLink Page, or catch us around at CSU. Weekly meetings take place @CSU campus. Will post location here: check back Fall 2019.

We are actively seeking new members to help our community grow. Now is the time to get involved! Come join us on Discord and expand your network.
